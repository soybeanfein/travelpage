Visit Yunnan, China

Explore the diverse culture, beautiful mountains, and rich history

Attractions

Kunming

Kunming, the capital of Yunnan Province, is known as the "City of Eternal Spring" for its mild climate year-round and is famous for its rich ethnic diversity and beautiful landscapes, such as the Stone Forest.

Tengchong

Food

Guo Qiao Mi Xian - Crossing the Bridge Rice Noodles

Guo Qiao Mi Xian literally translates into crossing the bridge rice noodles, and is an iconic Yunnan dish. The noodles are served in a hot pot, and the sides surround the pot in small dishes (like in the photo). These sides include meat, vegetables, quail eggs, etc. and they are one by one dropped into the pot. I think the aesthetic appeal of the dish, and being able to cook to toppings yourself really preserve the freshness of the food and bring an appreciation into the ingredients.

Xi Dou Fen (Porridge)

Xidoufen is a dish from Tengchong, my hometown in Yunnan, and a dish that brings me nostalgia and joy. The name directly translates to happy bean powder, as the porridge is made from grinded yellow peas and boiled with water. The thick porridge is eaten with ersi, thin rice noodles, or baba, a Chinese panfried flatbread (like Naan), and like most Yunnan cuisine, seasonings shine in Xidoufen. The porridge is usually seasoned with chilli oil, soy sauce, cilantro, green onions, etc. which makes a flavorful porridge that the noodles can be mixed with or the bread can be dipped into.

Er Kuai (Fried Rice Cake)

Er Kuai is another dish home to Yunnan. It is made from steamed rice that is pounded into a smooth dough, cut into thick pieces, and boiled. It's chewy, and delicious when fried with sauces and seasonings. I love savoury Er Kuai with meat and green onions!

Travel Tips!

  1. Install Chinese apps like WeChat, Alipay, Xiaohongshu, etc. these apps can help with booking taxis, making payments, and contacting people! [Install these apps in the playstore]

  2. Dress in layers! Especially during the rainy season in summer, Yunnan can go from sunny, to pouring, to windy, to humid. It's important to dress in layers to quickly change based on the weather, I like wearing windbreakers.
